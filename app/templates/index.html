How the percentages work

Ticket compliance measures only the selected top-level Jira ticket. Hierarchy compliance measures that ticket, all linked Epics and Stories, plus the hierarchy controls. Rolled-up story points totals the mapped Story Points field across the parent ticket, linked Epics and linked Stories; it is informational and does not change compliance scoring. Manager approval remains locked until the full hierarchy is compliant.
