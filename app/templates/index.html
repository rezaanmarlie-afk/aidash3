How the percentages work

Ticket compliance measures only the selected top-level Jira ticket. Hierarchy compliance measures that ticket, all linked Epics and Stories, plus the hierarchy controls. Rolled-up story points totals the mapped Story Points field across the parent ticket, linked Epics, linked Stories and any additional descendant delivery work found through Jira parent/Epic hierarchy or hierarchy-like links; it is informational and does not change compliance scoring. Initiative size is derived from the final rolled-up Story Points total using the thresholds configured in Settings. Manager approval remains locked until the full hierarchy is compliant.
